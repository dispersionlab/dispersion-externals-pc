ECG ANALYZER: INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------


to use without a running ecg signal:

open ECG_Analyzer_Test.mxb
turn the switch to the right (select the input from ecg_signals)
click the green on
click load
select a float64 raw data file (ecg_normal_1/2 , TubeNarrow_1/2)
click play
after 60 seconds the data should be coming out.

if you do have live signal:

you must have the digitizer working.
turn the switch to the left (select input from change_range)
click the green on
click play
after 60 seconds the data should be coming out.
